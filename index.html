About

This project was established by a group of young people from different countries of the world, with the aim of protecting the environment. We assist the concerned authorities in the reforestation of the deforested forests. We also organize concerts and charitable events in all countries of the world and donate their proceeds to continue this project. We focus as a non-profit organization that strives to save the earth and keep it green.

Our Achievments

  • We have recently been able to save many forests and highlight other environmental problems.
  • We were also able, through the efforts of volunteers, to spread in more than fifty countries around the world.
  • We were able to deal quickly in putting out more than twenty cases of forest fires in North and South Africa.
  • We have also shared our experiences with many new charities, and we have also been able to forge partnerships with organizations that advocate the protection of endangered animals
